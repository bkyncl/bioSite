Learn more about Christian...

Christian Llinas grew up in Colombia with his family. Which he remembers fondly as his home and makes trips back to visit often. At the age of 16, he and his family moved to Miami, FL. It was during this time that he decided to enlist in the army at the age of 17. He served for a total of seven years, during which time he made many new friends and experienced a breadth of new cultures. He has noted that his time in the service is what gave him a desire for travel, which he still loves to this day. He enjoys seeing new places, meeting new people, and above all trying new foods.


After his time in the army he pursued higher education at Florida International where he majored in international relations and global studies. After his undergraduate studies were complete, he decided to pursue an MBA from Quantic Tech. Eventually, he moved to Denver, CO for a change of both climate and scenery. He is proud of his ability to have accomplished finishing his education while working.


Aside from these accomplishments, Christian is a fun-loving and adventurous individual. He greatly values family and friends. He seeks to be a source of support for those around him, which is exemplified by his aspiration to one day be able to purchase a house for his mother. He is fluent in both English and Spanish. He is an avid sports fan who loves soccer, football, and tennis among many other sports. In his free time, he loves to go on any adventure he can. He likes to go on long road trips and hiking to take in the scenery. He loves the arts as well and goes to museums and concerts whenever he has the chance.
