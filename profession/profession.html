Experience & Education

Christian served in the armed forces for seven years. After which, he pursued higher education at Florida International where he majored in international relations and global studies. After his undergraduate studies were complete, he decided to pursue an MBA from Quantic Tech. Eventually, he moved to Denver, CO for a change of both climate and scenery. He is proud of his ability to have accomplished finishing his education while studying and working full time. Christian is now a senior consultant at a consulting firm specializing in global trade law. His enjoys working both with his collegues and for his community. He strives to continue to learn and grow everyday.
